These are files made for Mario Kart Wii that display some info on screen.
Use these files when you are racing against a ghost, otherwise the values of some addresses are inaccurate.
The x,y and z positions don't work mushroom gorge.
The XYZ speed Doesn't work on some tracks(Coconut mall and Mushroom Gorge and maybe a few more)
